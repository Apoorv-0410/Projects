About CampusBytes

Our Story

CampusBytes was founded in 2025 by a group of hungry college students who were tired of long queues and order misplacements. So we gathered our top students and came up with a solution CampusBytes, because no engineering student should ever debug code on an empty stomach. Remember "Order reday faster than Shatabdi Express! Come collect before the hungry professor does."

Our Mission

To transform college canteens from chaotic battleground into smooth candy crush or food crush. We're determined to ensure students spend their time debating over "why RCB have 0 trophy" or watching reels instead of arguing about whose dosa is whose.

Campus Vibes

At CampusBytes, we take food seriously but not ourselves. We're the perfect blend of tech and tadka, we combine cutting-edge tech with classic Indian jugaad.
